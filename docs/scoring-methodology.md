# Scoring Methodology (Not Yet Implemented)

Star ratings, a numeric "Change Score," and a declared "Winner" on
comparison pages are all judgment calls dressed up as data unless backed by
a disclosed, calculable formula. Shipping any of them without one is the
same problem as an unexplained star rating on a financial-information site
— it looks authoritative without being defensible.

All three stay out of the product until a scoring formula is defined,
published, and linked from a "How we calculate this" page. The current
plan is effective value at a standard monthly spend basket (e.g.
₹25,000/month), normalized against the category average — but this is not
final and nothing in the codebase should reference a score until it is.
